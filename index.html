Introduction

Hello, my name is Gabriel Spaer, but you can call me Aril. I would like to share a bit about my life with you.

At the age of 6, I started my formal education at SDN 2 KEMA. My elementary school years were filled with memories of learning and playing with friends. Over those 6 years, I learned many foundational skills and knowledge. After graduating from elementary school, I continued my education at a private junior high school, MTS Nurul Huda Kauditan. Junior high school was an important transitional period where I began to understand more about myself and my interests in various subjects.

After graduating from junior high, I went on to study at SMAN 1 Airmadidi for my high school education. High school was the most enjoyable and memorable period of my life. There, I met many kind and supportive friends. We spent a lot of time together, studying, playing, and planning our futures. Each day was colorful with various extracurricular activities, group projects, and school events that brought us closer together. However, after 3 years of cherished moments, we had to part ways to pursue our individual dreams and goals. Some of my friends chose paths to become doctors, engineers, and various other professions.

Currently, I am continuing my academic journey as a student at Politeknik Negeri Manado, majoring in Engineering. These college years are also filled with challenges and new learning experiences. I feel fortunate to learn from experienced lecturers and to meet new friends who share a strong passion for learning. Every day at the campus is an opportunity to deepen my knowledge and skills that will be useful in the future.

Looking ahead, after graduating from college, I aspire to become a UI/UX Designer. This field greatly interests me because it allows me to combine creativity and technical skills to create intuitive and appealing user interfaces. I hope to contribute to the development of digital products that are both useful and enjoyable to use. With determination and dedication, I am confident that I can achieve this dream and make a positive impact in the tech world.
